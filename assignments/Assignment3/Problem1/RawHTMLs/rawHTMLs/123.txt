Quick to make— no-bake! Easy to take-n’-go. Add some flair to your super-bowl party with this easy recipe that will make peanut butter and chocolate lovers craving to know how they are made.</div> <div class="divERSHeadItems"> <div class="ERSAuthor">Author: <span itemprop="author">Alexzandra O'Donnell</span></div> <div class="ERSCategory">Recipe type: <span itemprop="recipeCategory">Cookie</span></div> <div class="ERSServes">Serves: <span itemprop="recipeYield">10</span></div> </div> <div class="ERSIngredients"> <div class="ERSIngredientsHeader ERSHeading">Ingredients</div> <ul> <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">7 oz box of original Ritz Crackers</li> <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">16 oz jar of smooth Peanut Butter ( I used Jiff)</li> <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">Two 12 oz packages of Hersey’s Milk Chocolate Chips</li> <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">½ cup of Crisco (shortening)</li> <li class="ingredient" itemprop="ingredients">Optional: Decorative toppers or writing frosting</li> <li class="ERSIngredientImage"><a href="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/?attachment_id=30838"><img src="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Big-Game-Chocolate-Champs-ingredients_1.jpg" width="700" height="537" class="alignleft size-full" title="Big Game Chocolate Champs ingredients_1" alt="Big Game Chocolate Champs ingredients" /></a></li> </ul> <div class="ERSClear"></div> </div> <div class="ERSInstructions"> <div class="ERSInstructionsHeader ERSHeading">Instructions</div> <ol> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Cover a cooking pan with aluminum foil, preferably a shallow cookie sheet.</li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Frost a Ritz cracker evenly with peanut butter. When a pan is completed, place in the fridge to cool</li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Pour 2 packages of chocolate chips into a microwave safe bowl. Continue in 30 second increments until completely melted.</li> <li class="ERSInstructionImage"><a href="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/?attachment_id=30824"><img src="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Big-Game-Chocolate-Champs-preparation.jpg" width="3180" height="2850" class="alignleft size-full" title="Big Game Chocolate Champs preparation" alt="Big Game Chocolate Champs preparation" /></a></li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Remove a tray of the prepared Ritz and peanut butter sandwiches from the fridge. Dip each sandwich one-by-one into your bowl of chocolate, completely covering the sandwich. Place back on tray to cool.</li> <li class="ERSInstructionImage"><a href="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/?attachment_id=30825"><img src="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Big-Game-Chocolate-Champs-preparation-2_1.jpg" width="700" height="447" class="alignleft size-full" title="Big Game Chocolate Champs preparation 2_1" /></a></li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">If placing decorative toppers onto cookies, place when chocolate is still slightly melted. When the cookies are chilled, they are simple to pop into a to-go container or onto a serving tray.</li> <li class="ERSInstructionImage"><a href="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/?attachment_id=30826"><img src="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Big-Game-Chocolate-Champs_1.jpg" width="700" height="511" class="alignleft size-full" title="Big Game Chocolate Champs_1" alt="Big Game Chocolate Champs" /></a></li> </ol> <div class="ERSClear"></div> </div> <div class="endeasyrecipe" title="style002" style="display: none">3.2.2925</div> </div><br />
