    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p><a href="http://www.tmz.com/2016/02/09/lesean-mccoy-bar-fight-video-meek-mill/" target="_blank">TMZ Sports</a> has obtained video of what they say is the altercation at Recess Lounge in Old City Philadelphia involving Buffalo Bills running back LeSean McCoy and a number of off-duty Philly police officers.</p>
<p>The incident was believed to be sparked over an argument over a bottle of champagne. You can <a href="http://www.csnphilly.com/football-philadelphia-eagles/lesean-mccoy-curtis-brinkley-altercation-duty-police" target="_blank">read our original story on the altercation including details from the police report right here</a>.</p>

It appears LeSean throws at least one punch during the melee."</p>
<p>The person who shot the video and gave it to TMZ also told them that Shady McCoy was particularly rowdy.</p>
<p class="p1"><span style="font-size: 13.008px; line-height: 1.538em;">Law enforcement sources told CSNPhilly.com's John Gonzalez this morning that the </span><span style="font-size: 13.008px; line-height: 1.538em;">warrant process started last night and should be submitted to the DA for approval today.
