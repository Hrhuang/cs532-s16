				<h2>After a campaign rally in New Hampshire, Senator <span class="scoreregex" data-GovTrackId="412491"  data-Title="Sen"  data-First="Marco"  data-Last="Rubio"  data-Party="R"  data-State="FL"  data-Grade="C"  data-Average="79"  data-Link="marco-rubio/?utm_campaign=breitbartapi&amp;utm_medium=api&amp;utm_source=breitbart"  data-Image="412491.jpeg" >Sen. Marco Rubio (R-FL)</span> revealed to a voter what kind of guns he and his wife own.</h2>
<p>Rubio was approached by a man wearing a T-shirt with former president Ronald Reagan flipping the bird. The man asked to have his picture taken with the senator.</p>
<p>“Gun question. Glock or 1911?” the man asked as the two posed for the cameras.</p>
<p>“I don’t have either,” Rubio replied. “I have a Taurus .357 and my wife got a Smith &amp; Wesson — the Bodyguard .380,” he replied.</p>
<p>Rubio’s comments were aired on CSPAN as cameras followed him while he greeted supporters after his rally in Nashua last night.</p>
<p>“It’s been a long time up here for you, hasn’t it,” the man said.</p>
<p>“Wow. It has, but it’s been fun,” Rubio replied.</p>
<p>The Reagan t-shirt was pointed out on Twitter by someone who was at the rally.</p>

<p>“Look at that t-shirt,” Rubio chuckled after he saw the shirt.</p>
<p>“Ben Carson loved this t-shirt,” the man replied. “You don’t know how many press people have stopped me tonight over this t-shirt.”</p>
