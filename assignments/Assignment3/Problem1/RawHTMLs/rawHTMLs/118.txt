                                        <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items" itemprop="articleBody"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>Duke quarterback Thomas Sirk sustained a ruptured left Achilles during a team workout on Tuesday morning, the school <a href="https://twitter.com/Duke_FB/status/697099020997693442" target="_blank">announced</a>.</p>

<p>There is no timetable for his return, but he is expected to undergo surgery on Wednesday.</p>

<p>Sirk missed the 2013 with a ruptured right Achilles.</p>

<p>As a junior last year, Sirk started 12 games and recorded 3,428 total yards, the second most in a single season by a Blue Devils quarterback. Parker Boehme, who started one game last year, may fill in Sirk’s absence if the quarterback misses extended time during the 2016 season.</p>

<p>Duke finished last season 8–5 with a victory in the Pinstripe Bowl over the Indiana Hoosiers.</p>
