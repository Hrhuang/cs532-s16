All is Lost- Mobile Game Pick of the Week

In this arcade game by Foursaken Media mankind is spread across the universe struggling to survive the harsh realities of space. All is Lost is a rhythm runner game with great imagery and availability on your mobile.

AllenJones2015
This is absolutely gorgeous and f2p done as it should be. Ads are already gone and having a blast. I inverted the controls as for me, the swipes to jump/roll feel more natural on the right. Congrats and well done.
source : toucharcade.com
All is Lost - Game Trailer

All is Lost was released on February 4 by Foursaken Media and has been receiving favorable reviews from players.

The premise is simple, your crew members must evacuate deteriorating space stations to escape pods before it's too late. By swiping the screen you can help them dodge and roll out of the way of dangerous debris.

source : toucharcade.com

Timing is key to avoiding incineration.

The scenery and visuals in All is Lost are great, the soundtrack also fitting the setting and adding to the experience.

source : toucharcade.com

Lead your crew through different locations, leaping to safety and traversing dangerous constructions.

This game is really good for the runner genre. It's not infinite, really, but it's very good. I am amazed that the game is free, and all the purchases they ask for $1 to remove all ads.
source : App Store

Currently, this title is available for free on the App store for iPhone and costs $1 to remove ads from the game. Give it a try!
