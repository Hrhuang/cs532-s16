Women's Basketball Player of the Week - February 9, 2016

Lynzi Mueller, Carlow University

Senior • Guard• Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

This week’s USCAA Women’s Division I Basketball Player of the Week honor goes Lynzi Mueller of Carlow University. Against Rio Grande, Mueller led the Celtics with 20 points (6-14 FG's) (3-6 three-point FG's) and 5-6 from the charity stripe. She also pulled down four boards and had two assists to go along with two steals. This past Saturday the Celtics avenged a 20 point loss earlier in the season to Ohio Christian with a 64-57 win. In the victory, Mueller scored 17 points, grabbed five boards, dished out two assists and also tallied two steals. Carlow University is now 11-11 on the season and ranked seventh in the latest USCAA Coaches Poll.
