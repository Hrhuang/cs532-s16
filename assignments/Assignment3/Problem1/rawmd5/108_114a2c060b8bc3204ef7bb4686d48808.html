NBC catches Leicester fever in new video to promote Sunday’s game vs. Arsenal

Posted on February 9, 2016 by Christopher Harris
Christopher Harris 6 days ago

NBC Sports has released a promotional video to build up the momentum ahead of Sunday’s Premier League match between Arsenal and Leicester City.

The Foxes take on the Gunners in a game that will be televised live across the United States on NBCSN beginning at 7am ET (pre-match coverage begins at 6am ET).

NBC’s new promo video is entitled ‘Who’s Laughing Now?’

After Sunday’s result, we’ll have a much clearer picture.
