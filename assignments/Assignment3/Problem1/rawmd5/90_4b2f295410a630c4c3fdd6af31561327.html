San Antonio Spurs vs. Miami Heat

Spurs: 43-8 SU,33-18 ATS
Heat: 29-23 SU,26-25-1 ATS
When: 8:00 PM ET, Tuesday, February 9, 2016
Where: American Airlines Arena, Miami, Florida
TV: TNT, FSN Southwest

The San Antonio Spurs begin their annual Rodeo Road Trip on Tuesday when they visit the Miami Heat in the first of eight consecutive road tilts.

The AT&T Center is unavailable due to the San Antonio Stock Show & Rodeo, so the Spurs won't play at home again until March 2.

San Antonio is 28-0 at home but its road record is pretty stellar as well at 15-8, second-best in the NBA behind the Golden State Warriors.

MyBookie.ag has the Spurs listed as 7-point favorites and the game's total is sitting at 193.
