2.0 out of 5 starsNot 15 liters by any means.
By Jay Perrotto on April 17, 2015
Color: Orange Verified Purchase
There were reviews about this pack being smaller than it appears but they did not do its lack of volume justice. The pack is absolutely well made and no expense appears to have been spared on quality or attention to detail. However, this is not nearly 15l by any stretch. I'd be surprised if it's even 10l. I shopped 15l bags in person before ordering this pack and it does not come near the size of any pack I handled. I don't know if it compares apples to apples but my 2 liter water bladder fit with zero room to spare. Many people recommended this for day hiking. I don't know what they consider a day hike but I couldn't fit a fraction of the equipment I bring on a good hike. Rain gear, packable jacket, food, water, emergency bivvy, etc. The lack of size is unworkable to begin with but the side zipper as opposed to a top loader makes me wonder why this bag exists at all. To pack or access anything from the side is foolishly awkward. If you want somewhere to put your phone, sunglasses and keys buy cargo shorts.

4.0 out of 5 starsGreat option for a day hike!
By Karen B. on April 20, 2015
Color: Black Verified Purchase
I needed a waterproof backpack for walking around a city with a lot of precipitation and also for glacier hiking. Based on other reviews, I was already expecting it to be on the smaller side but it was even smaller than I was expecting. With that being said, the backpack was still able to hold everything that I needed for the day. I think the most I was able to fit in it was a rolled up scarf, a rolled up long sleeve women's shirt, rolled up waterproof women's pants, a small wallet, and a small zippered bag for miscellaneous small items. My water bottle was held on the side outer pocket. I used this bag in the rain and snow and the contents in the main large compartment were kept dry; however, the items in the two small zipper pockets on the front got wet. It appears that although the material is waterproof, the zippers are not. This was not a big deal to me since I didn't store anything in those pockets that were really important but it is definitely something to keep in mind. The backpack is very comfortable to wear and the size prevents you from overloading the bag, which keeps it light. The shape of the bag maintains a smaller profile and it doesn't protrude so far from the body. The fabric seems durable and doesn't look like it would tear easily.

Overall, this is a great backpack for a day hike. I must say it was a little different from what I was expecting but it turned out to be a great choice for my activities. Although the backpack wasn't completely waterproof due to the zippers, I would still recommend this bag and enjoyed using it on my trip.
