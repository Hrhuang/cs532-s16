Draymond Green Pours Water on Stephen Curry After 51-Point Game (VIDEO)

February 03, 2016 By SLAM Staff

Stephen Curry dropped 51 points, making the most ridiculous shots, in a 134-121 win over the Wizards on Wednesday. Curry was so en fuego that Draymond Green had to douse him in water after game.

Green didn’t have a bad game himself: 12 points, 10 rebounds, 12 assists for his 10th triple-double this season.
