The Breakfast Club – Game Jam Build Download

   Posted on [11]February 9, 2016 by [12]KJ Robertson

   [13]the breakfast club

   The Breakfast Club is a very silly game in which you and up to 3 other
   friends possess everyday objects, such as knives, plates and bread,
   then attempt to make breakfast.

   The current build of The Breakfast Club was created for Global Game Jam
   2016, and features one level (called ‘Fear & Loafing’) in which you and
   your friends attempt to toast and butter 6 slices of bread. Needless to
   say this is a tricky task and wonderfully chaotic – too many cooks
   really do spoil the broth (or toast), but it’s great fun witnessing the
   carnage in 4 player multiplayer.

   Making breakfast has never been so tricky, but it’s great fun. With a
   bit of perseverance and a lot of luck, maybe you’ll eventually get that
   toast buttered. We can’t wait to see the new level the devs are working
   on, ‘Fry Hard’, there’s nothing like a good cooked breakfast!

   Note:  Struggling to get the toast in the toaster?  You’ll need to
   construct some sort of a ramp…
