   Watch: TMZ video of alleged Shady McCoy bar fight in Philly
   February 9, 2016, 12:00 pm
   Share This Post
   [26]Comments
   [27][byline-enrico-campitelli.jpg]

   IFRAME:
   [28]//platform.twitter.com/widgets/follow_button.html?screen_name=the70
   0level&show_count=false

   [29]TMZ Sports has obtained video of what they say is the altercation
   at Recess Lounge in Old City Philadelphia involving Buffalo Bills
   running back LeSean McCoy and a number of off-duty Philly police
   officers.

   The incident was believed to be sparked over an argument over a bottle
   of champagne. You can [30]read our original story on the altercation
   including details from the police report right here.

   IFRAME:
   [31]//cdnapisec.kaltura.com/p/591531/sp/59153100/embedIframeJs/uiconf_i
   d/6740162/partner_id/591531?iframeembed=true&playerId=kaltura_player_14
   13478522&entry_id=0_5vqr2ozj

   As for what you can see in the TMZ video, [32]they claim you can see
   McCoy throwing at least one punch, "In the video, you can see several
   men punching each other at Recess Lounge in Philly early Sunday
   morning. It appears LeSean throws at least one punch during the melee."

   The person who shot the video and gave it to TMZ also told them that
   Shady McCoy was particularly rowdy.

   Law enforcement sources told CSNPhilly.com's John Gonzalez this morning
   that the warrant process started last night and should be submitted to
   the DA for approval today. Warrants could be issued by Wednesday
   morning.
