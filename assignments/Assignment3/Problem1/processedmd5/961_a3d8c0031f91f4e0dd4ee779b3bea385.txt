my halo mcc my love hate relationship

   Hi all,
   First of I enjoy halo, have done from day one on the Xbox.
   campaign whether solo or co op. Have had some great laughs in co op and
   multi player. So just had to get halo mcc on release day.
   But from day one I had problems with halo mcc. In the beginning I was
   playing campaigns doing bit MP etc.
   I had the usual problems connecting, disconnecting, crashes etc in MP.
   Single player I was doing bit n pieces in campaigns sometimes I would
   have glitches so got stuck or enemies were invincible etc etc. My main
   bug was I'd done halo 2 legendary and nothing popped including most of
   the levels. I was so annoyed I left game on shelf and played some of my
   many other games.
   Now then ODST launched and as it was free and I did enjoy it first time
   around.(wish firefight was included). I decided to start playing it
   again.
   Initially had no problems it looked good and played well. First had
   problems in co op doing déjà vu. Lag, engineer getting stuck and the
   game freezing for everyone right at the very end. Luckily we had good
   group who like me laugh it off and just did it again.
   Another time we were doing LASO and it was nigh impossible due to lag,
   disconnects, glitches and crashes (usually at end of level just as you
   see the end).
   Now I must admit levels and miscellaneous achievements all popped when
   they should do.... but then I did the audio log run through I got all
   29 (or so I thought) went to data hive got no.30 and got the
   achievement for it but not one for getting all the audio logs? So I
   double checked everything and found one audio log with the dreaded DONE
   UNLOCKING, it been like that for about fortnight now. So missing two
   achievements because of this. I'm just hopping it'll pop at some point
   and unlock both. Anyway that's ODST till we try laso again.
   Now last weekend had huge session on Saturday and got about 30
   achievements in halo 3. Session was five hours but ended up lasting 9
   hours... anyway all was good in the halo world.... until Sunday.
   Now on Sunday we had another session for halo 4 we were doing legendary
   co op.
   It started badly and got worse, first level, terminals score/time
   nothing popped for me but did for the others. This went on for mission
   after mission. I got sick of answering no nothing popped.
   The other guys were all getting their achievements but not me waaaaah
   waaaaaah.
   I tried hosting, we all tried hosting, I hard reset, I reset router etc
   etc..no difference.
   So once we done the campaign on legendary
   Two guys had everything they should
   One guy was missing a level, a terminal..just daft odd ones.
   And me guess what I got...1 achievement for the hidden Easter egg.
   I had checked my achievements, there was no % progress, no done
   unlocking
   But on the missions it showed I'd completed them all co op on
   legendary.
   I checked halo waypoint... nothing. No levels complete no scores or
   times. All my other halo service records were there....I checked later
   and there was nothing... no service record. It said I'd never played
   the game. Checked later on in single player on halo 4 said I'd done
   four levels on normal but nothing in co op.
   Later again co op showed legendary for four levels no scores but every
   par time was the same... wrong.
   So anyway checked the forums and I'm not alone, there were a few on
   waypoint and a permanent link advising what to do... the first part was
   wait 72 hours.
   So Tues night/wed morn I was watching E3 daily show and achievements
   started popping, I checked them and I got about 12 or so, some levels,
   terminals, scores, times and completing campaign.
   Now strange thing was I got normal and heroic but no easy and
   legendary. I got the one for all terminals but had not got a couple of
   terminals in halo 4. There were a few anomalies like that.
   Then today Thursday achievements started popping again, it seems it was
   the rest of the ones missing from Sunday...I think.
   Got easy legendary terminals etc...thank you Xbox.
   Halo waypoint is still not up to date.
   Just need my audio log to unlock.... fingers crossed.
   P.S on a side note spent two hours last night trying to rocket jump to
   get the scarab gun...I was flying off hitting underneath the bridge,
   zooming off into space looking at the bridge miles below me or bloody
   blowing myself up.
   And today don't get me started about halo 2 IWHBYD skull today....
   bloody random chance 1-7 my arse, must have taken fifty attempts
   And as we all do we keep coming back for more.... that's a gamer for
   you.
   Oh well more halo this weekend.... at least I've got batman for next
   week
