   Super Bowl MVP [127]Von Miller sent a postgame message to [128]Johnny
   Manziel on Sunday night:

   [129]I Love you fam! Grind, get back to Johnny all the ags know and
   love, Grind bro, 2yrs ago they counted me out, 2yrs frm now you can do
   the same!! Support no matter what! #love @jmanziel2

   A photo posted by Von Miller (@millerlite40) on Feb 7, 2016 at 11:04pm
   PST

   Manziel was quick to respond:

   [130]Champ @millerlite40

   A photo posted by Johnny Manziel (@jmanziel2) on Feb 7, 2016 at 11:50pm
   PST

   Miller and Manziel both played at Texas A&M. Miller made a similar
   statement to TV cameras after the game, "Hey, Johnny Man, I know it's a
   hard time. I've been in the same situation. I'm here with you, buddy.
   Keep grinding, and you, too, can be here in two more years. I love
   you." Miller refered to his six-game suspension in 2013 for violating
   the NFL's substance abuse policy.

   Manziel is the subject of a Dallas police investigation into a
   complaint filed by Colleen Crowley that he assaulted her the evening of
   Jan. 29.

   Last week a judge granted Crowley's request for a protection order,
   ruling Manziel cannot see Crowley for two years and that he must stay
   500 feet from her home and workplace.

   Manziel told TMZ the assault "did not happen."
