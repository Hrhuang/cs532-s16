Customer Reviews

The game is below average


   by Coldemoalex

   The game is fun dont. Get me wrong, the graphics are beautiful and the
   atmosphere is spectacular, theres some types of fish and lures which
   makes the game fun but the big down fall is that the videos for more
   coins and special gifts are broken none existent. It relies souly on a
   slow progress, no way to get silver and funds on micro transactions. I
   get buying fishing license but daily?? Remove that limit since theres
   no actual place to get silver and its way over excessive for people who
   dont play that often thats the mayor downfall for the game. Fix the
   videos, get more silver for challenges and alternative ways to earn it,
   and remove the licenses you can only make people buy one and its open
   for ever. If that isnt fixed then the game will not be fun and be
   forgotten

Rapala fishing game like being there


   by Cmedbdis

   Wow! This game is uncanny
   in it's understanding and real time fishing action. Lots of options and
   I love the chances to gain funds throughout... The graphics are
   beautiful and I can't wait to find more fishing holes as I move along
   since the one at the Excelsior Dock on Lake Minnetonka is a perfect
   representation! Really satisfying and I've never even been a fisherman.

Needs Fixing and more options.


   by TheAHero

   This game is great for only a little while. There is plenty of bugs,
   and hopefully those get fixed. There is also a lot of balancing issues,
   but also not enough content. If you can add more content (more fish,
   better AI, more locations, more ways to earn more coins, etc.) this
   game will skyrocket. Fix these things in the next updates, and this
   game will be a success. Don't, this game might be a bust.
