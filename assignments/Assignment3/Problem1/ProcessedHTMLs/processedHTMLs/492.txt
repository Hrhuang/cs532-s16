   Treasure Pop - Free Bubble Shooter is the best bubble shooting puzzle
   adventure game!

   Throw bubble stones and match colored stones to pop and drop your
   treasures in this challenging bubble shooter game with addictive and
   intuitive gameplay and defeat the Evil Dwarf and recover your
   treasures.

   FEATURES
   - Action-packed bubble shooting puzzle gameplay with unlimited FREE
   Play and 300+ levels.
   - Easy pick up and play for fans of bubble shooter and puzzle games.
   - Match and pop stones to get your gems back from Evil Dwarf
   - Challenge friends and see their progress by connecting to Facebook
   - Enchanting graphics that will leave you spellbound
   - Special boosters & God powers to help you pass those tricky levels
   - Available to play on mobile and tablet devices

   HOW TO PLAY
   - Shoot, match and pop bubble stones of same color.
   - Fill God powers by popping same colored stones.
   - Use boosters to ease gameplay (Rainbow Stone, Super Aim, Extra Swap &
   Hammer of God)
   - Master tricky obstacles and use booster stones to do trick shots.
   - Use God Powers to finish levels! (Red, Blue, Yellow, Green and
   combine them to use Hammer of God).

   GOOD TO KNOW
   Free & easy to play, challenging to master! Treasure Pop is a 100% free
   bubble shooting game and requires no in-app purchases nor do we require
   you to connect to social networks in order to progress throughout the
   game. We do however allow our players to connect to social networks
   such as Facebook and Twitter to share scores.
