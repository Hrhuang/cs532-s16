Draymond Green Pours Water on Stephen Curry After 51-Point Game (VIDEO)

   February 03, 2016 By [104]SLAM Staff

   IFRAME: [105]https://vine.co/v/iJHIT9OMlVu/embed/simple

   [106]Stephen Curry dropped 51 points, making the most ridiculous shots,
   in a 134-121 win over the Wizards on Wednesday. Curry was so en fuego
   that Draymond Green had to douse him in water after game.

   Green didn’t have a bad game himself: 12 points, 10 rebounds, 12
   assists for his 10th triple-double this season.
