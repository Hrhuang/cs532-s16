   By [70]antisneakerhead -
   Feb 9, 2016

   For the past thirtysomething years, Air Jordans and NBA-All Star Games
   have gone together like black and red on a sneaker. It’s a timeless
   tradition, it never gets old (ok, it’s kinda old, but we won’t dare
   bring that up to sneakerheads) and we always look forward to what the
   next thing is, whether it’s the latest and greatest or a classic from
   the past brought back [DEL: because we can’t let go :DEL] (dammit,
   sorry…). Either way, a new chapter is added every year (with a few
   exceptions) and it’s something to look back on as the legend of the Air
   Jordan just continues to grow with each passing year. Here is a look
   back at the Air Jordans that have graced the NBA’s biggest and
   brightest event of the year.
