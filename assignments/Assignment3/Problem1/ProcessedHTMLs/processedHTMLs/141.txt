Cheer on the Lads this Saturday

   Published: 09 February, 2016
   by Kate Smith

   Tickets for Red Devils' visit still on sale.

   Sunderland AFC will host Manchester United at the Stadium of Light in
   the Barclays Premier League this weekend.

   Louis van Gaal’s men head to Wearside on Saturday 13 February, for a
   12.45pm kick-off.

   The Red Devils are without a win in their last two trips to the Stadium
   of Light after Jack Rodwell’s header secured a 1-1 draw last season and
   Fabio Borini’s penalty gave Sunderland a 2-1 win in the first leg of
   the Capital One Cup semi-final in 2014.

   Supporters wishing to purchase tickets for this game must have a valid
   customer number and purchase history with the club. Tickets are on sale
   now, priced from £27 for adults, £23 for over 65s and £12 for under
   16s.

   The stadium’s Fan Zone will be open before and after the game,
   featuring live music, entertainment and a range of food and beverage
   options for supporters wishing to soak up the atmosphere. The Fan Zone
   is located on the south east corner of the stadium.

   Tickets can still be purchased at the ticket office, [86]via the online
   ticketing service and the ticket hotline (0871 911 1973).
