Marcus Paige, UNC look for formula to break out of two-game slump

   Posted on [24]February 9, 2016 by [25]DLN Feed [26]Leave a comment

   If you think that a shooting slump results in a lot of advice only for
   that player, think again: Sometimes the training staff hears it, too.
   During Marcus Paige’s recent six-game stretch of struggles from the
   floor, an email landed with UNC’s medical people. It suggested they
   outfit Paige with a mouthpiece. Because a mouthpiece would relax entire
   his body. And that would fix him.
