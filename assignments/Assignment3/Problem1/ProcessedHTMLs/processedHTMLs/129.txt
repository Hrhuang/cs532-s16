@OspreyWTen Hits Road to face UCF

   ORLANDO, Fla. - North Florida women's tennis begins its five-game road
   swing at UCF on Wednesday at 2 p.m. The Ospreys are currently 2-1 on
   the season, while the Knights are 3-1.
   UNF is coming off a 7-0 sweep of Flagler on Friday, Feb. 5. Three
   players currently own two victories in singles play with freshman
   [193]Rafaela Gomez sitting at 2-0 in the No. 5 spot. Doubles partners
   [194]Noemi Hemmerich and [195]Quetzali Vazquez are each 2-1 in the No.
   1 and No. 3 singles positions, respectively. In doubles play, Gomez and
   [196]Luise Intert are 3-0 in No. 2 matches, while [197]Hannah Fluhrer
   and [198]Michelle Valdez own the same mark in No. 3.
   The Knights' Sofia Baptista and Emilia Granstrom are each 4-0 in No. 2
   and No. 3 singles, respectively, while two others have three victories.
   UCF opened with a Quad Tournament with Rice, North Texas and FIU before
   hosting Troy in its first dual match. The Knights lone loss is to
   Atlantic Sun Conference foe FGCU.
   North Florida owns a 5-4 record in the last nine meetings against UCF,
   but stand at an even 2-2. In the last two seasons, the Ospreys have
   edged the Knights, 4-3. UCF last won in 2013 by the same score.
   After UCF, the Ospreys continue on with a trip to Alabama at Auburn and
   Troy on February 20 and 21, respectively.
