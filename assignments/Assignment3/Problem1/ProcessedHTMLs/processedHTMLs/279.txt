   Gyrosphere. No one. I mean, if you feel like you can get over the

Customer Reviews

High quality changes


   by Cwolfe13524

   I wrote a review a while ago about how this game had a great idea but
   there were too many bugs and difficult levels to make it truly a good
   game. Well, I am really impressed with the developers, because they
   have made some major changes to the logistics of the game and now, it's
   one of my favorites of all time. Kudos.

Very amusing


   by Daniel-Owens

   I think this game would be more fun if it used the tilt of your phone
   but for what it is, I can't complain. The levels are very challenging.
   However, I wish it was easier to get the balls for free. To get free
   balls you have to either wait 24 hours (get 25) OR get 5 stars on a
   level and then beat your time (get 5).

Clunky controls and a knock off


   by riotquietly

   Pretty much a cheap knock off of "Aerox" without the good control
   system.
   How can you call a game gyros sphere and have it not utilize the
   gyroscope in the phone? Tilt control option should be the way to go.
   You should also be able to stop the ball instantly, control the camera
   independently from the ball, and lock the camera wherever. Pretty much
   everything I liked about "Aerox" is not in this.
   I gave it two stars because it is visually appealing.
