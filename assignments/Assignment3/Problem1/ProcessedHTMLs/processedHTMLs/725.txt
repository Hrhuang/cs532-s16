Assassin's Creed Chronicles: Russia

Assassin’s Creed Chronicles: Russia Launch Trailer Also Touts Three-Game
Bundle

   [70]Preview
   by [71]Mike Futter on February 09, 2016 at 10:00 AM
   5,814 Views
   [72][pc-icon.png]
   [73]4

   Ubisoft has wrapped up its 2D experimental Assassin’s Creed series with
   today’s launch of Chronicles: Russia. With the arc now complete, you
   can also purchase a retail bundle featuring all three games.

   The series has taken us around the globe to new playable locations. You
   can journey through 1526 China with Shao Jun (featured in Assassin's
   Creed: Embers), 1841 India with Arbaaz Mir (the focus of Assassin's
   Creed: Brahman), and 1918 Russia with Nikolaï Orelov (star of the
   Assassin’s Creed: The Fall comic).

   You can read [74]our review of the India installment for more.
   Assassin’s Creed Chronicles: Russia is out today for PlayStation 4,
   Xbox One, and PC.
