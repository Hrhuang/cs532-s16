<h2 class='date-header'><span>miércoles, 6 de abril de 2016</span></h2>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry uncustomized-post-template' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>
<meta content='https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-XTBFbHrku4A/VwVR7ZIxsgI/AAAAAAAACS0/3p4mMn1e96wNeyyV5qhysQaZJTdLrkx4w/s400/HeronOblivion_2015_promo_01_Photo_AlissaAnderson_2203x1500_300.jpg' itemprop='image_url'/>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://kistefm.blogspot.com/2016/04/heron-oblivion.html'>Heron Oblivion</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5224319920880169938' itemprop='description articleBody'>
&nbsp;<br />
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-XTBFbHrku4A/VwVR7ZIxsgI/AAAAAAAACS0/3p4mMn1e96wNeyyV5qhysQaZJTdLrkx4w/s1600/HeronOblivion_2015_promo_01_Photo_AlissaAnderson_2203x1500_300.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><img border="0" height="271" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-XTBFbHrku4A/VwVR7ZIxsgI/AAAAAAAACS0/3p4mMn1e96wNeyyV5qhysQaZJTdLrkx4w/s400/HeronOblivion_2015_promo_01_Photo_AlissaAnderson_2203x1500_300.jpg" width="400" /></a></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><b>Heron Oblivion: manzanas traigo.</b></td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<br />
<b>&nbsp; H</b>ace poco más de un mes <a href="https://www.subpop.com/artists/heron_oblivion"><b>Sub Pop</b></a> editó el que probablemente sea el mejor debut del año. Un combo de artistas de San Francisco, salidos de bandas como <i><b>Comets on Fire</b> </i>o <b><i>Espers</i></b>, han unido sus fuerzas e inspiración para formar <b>Heron Oblivion</b>. En su primer disco de presentación, el cuarteto nos deleita con una psicodelia pastoral que se mueve entre la calma y el espasmo. Melodías que evocan paisajes vastos y amplios cargados de arena, pero también sensualidad.&nbsp; En muchos de sus pasajes se hace inevitable mirar de reojo a los excepcionales <b><i>Cowboy Junkies</i></b>, en especial cuando la voz de Meg Baird acuna la parte instrumental emitida por el resto de la banda: <span class="data"><span itemprop="description">Ethan Miller, Noel Von Harmonson, and Charlie Saufley. Agreste y cálido como una postal de un verano perdido, así son Heron Oblivion. Escúchalos y disfruta de su grandeza.</span></span><br />
<span class="data"><span itemprop="description"><br /></span></span>
<span class="data"><span itemprop="description">Javier Mateos</span></span><br />

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry uncustomized-post-template' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>
<meta content='https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-id2gppj05e4/VrdXQO5ZDXI/AAAAAAAACRI/R00jwjVnYEs/s400/shearwater-640x450.jpg' itemprop='image_url'/>
<meta content='4766319440427533377' itemprop='blogId'/>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://kistefm.blogspot.com/2016/02/shearwater-backchannels.html'>Shearwater: Backchannels</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-7379816369975430849' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-id2gppj05e4/VrdXQO5ZDXI/AAAAAAAACRI/R00jwjVnYEs/s1600/shearwater-640x450.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><img border="0" height="281" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-id2gppj05e4/VrdXQO5ZDXI/AAAAAAAACRI/R00jwjVnYEs/s400/shearwater-640x450.jpg" width="400" /></a></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;">Shearwater: el último poeta.</td></tr>
</tbody></table>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
Cuando todos pensábamos que la Poesía había muerto, llegaron tus versos.</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</div>
<div style="text-align: left;">
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<span style="font-size: x-small;"> Javier Mateos</span></div>
<br />
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><b>READ:</b></span></div>
<div class="lyrics-body" style="text-align: center;">
<div class="js-lyric-text invalid-selection" id="lyrics-body-text">
<div class="verse">
Oh, when you whistle down the wind<br />
When you needle the dark with your mind<br />
When you're firing volleys of words in an arc<br />
When you're snuffing out all that's still alive</div>
<div class="verse">
And a droplet falls<br />
From the dropper's eye<br />
Blooms like a wave<br />
That slowly overruns all of your days<br />
And slips the caul<br />
From off your eyes<br />
You face alone<br />
A fear that's dragging us all in its wake</div>
<div class="verse">
And you kill the lights</div>
<div class="verse">
I know<br />
You're in the river now<br />
And you yield to the water's embrace<br />
And you lie like a stone on the banks, giving out<br />
As it carries off all of our names</div>
<div class="verse">
I know that sound<br />
I know what it likes<br />
I know it feels<br />
Like all the guns of a battery trained<br />
Right at your eyes</div>
<div class="verse">
Because it's real<br />
Because it pulls<br />
A thread of slowly unraveling days<br />
Annihilates<br />
Your mother tongue<br />
Your only light</div>
<div class="verse">
But hold this sound<br />
Follow it all the way</div>
<div class="verse">
And put down the knife</div>
<div class="verse">
The night is here<br />
But still is spinning out stars in its wake<br />
And that stubborn light<br />
Pools in your heart<br />
Warm and nacreous, baby<br />
The milk of sighs<br />
And dreams</div>
<div class="verse">
<br /></div>

<a href='http://kistefm.blogspot.com/2016/01/bill-fay-be-not-so-fearful.html'>Bill Fay: Be Not So Fearful </a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4034832745981330503' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<span style="color: #20124d;"><b>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</b></span><br />
<span style="color: #20124d;"><b>&nbsp;<table align="center" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" class="tr-caption-container" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; text-align: center;"><tbody>
<tr><td style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-pztFF-OtPbQ/VqkgsNSQhUI/AAAAAAAACQ0/I5eBSsPpdbQ/s1600/Bill-Fay1.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><img border="0" height="400" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-pztFF-OtPbQ/VqkgsNSQhUI/AAAAAAAACQ0/I5eBSsPpdbQ/s400/Bill-Fay1.png" width="397" /></a></td></tr>
<tr><td class="tr-caption" style="text-align: center;"><b>Bill Fay: Caminante no hay camino.</b></td></tr>

</b></span><br />
<span style="color: #20124d;"><br /></span>
<span style="color: #20124d;"><b>&nbsp;&nbsp; N</b></span>o te pongas nervioso a pesar de que al levantarte no sientas las piernas. No te preocupes si tu espalda no responde a la primera. Ten la calma suficiente para mirar de vez en cuando a las estrellas.<br />
<br />
Javier Mateos<br />
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-size: large;"><b><span style="color: #20124d;">READ:</span></b></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">

Be not so nervous, be not so frail<br />
Someone watches you, you will not fail<br />
Be not so nervous, be not so frail<br />
Be not so nervous, be not so frail<br />
<br />
Be not so sorry for what you've done<br />
You must forget them now, it's done<br />
And when you wake up you will find you can run<br />
Be not so sorry for what you've done<br />
<br />
Be not so fearful, be not so pale<br />
Someone watches you, you will not leave the rails<br />
Be not so fearful, be not so pale<br />
Be not so fearful, be not so pale<br />
<br />
Be not so sorry for what you've done<br />
You must forget them now, it's done<br />
And when you wake up you will find you can run<br />
Be not so sorry for what you've done</div>
