No need to copy or upload images.Click inside the text box, click Ctrl  A, then Ctrl C and then Ctrl V on your post's html page.Trust me, it's  easy!<br /><br />This tag is for the first photo.<br /><textarea><a href="http://bogglemethursday.blogspot.com/"><img src="http://i379.photobucket.com/albums/oo233/MommyW_2008/December%202010/d290eb46.jpg" border="0" alt="week_73_1-1" /><br /></a></textarea><br /><br />This tag is for the second photo.<br /><textarea><br /><br /><a href="http://bogglemethursday.blogspot.com/"><img src="http://i379.photobucket.com/albums/oo233/MommyW_2008/December%202010/27f3e90a.jpg" border="0" alt="week_73_2-1" /><br /></a></textarea><br /><br />For the specific rules to Boggle, please go <a href="http://bogglemethursday.blogspot.com/2008/10/boggle-me-thusday.html">here</a>.   But remember, this is for fun; there are no points, no winner or  losers!If you don't have a blog, feel free to list your words in the  comments.<br /><br />For an added twist, when you're done, write a sentence  with as many of the words you found as you can!Please remember to visit  fellow Bogglers... part of the fun is seeing what words others found!<br /><span style="font-weight: bold;"><br />Please be sure to link the actual post where you played... posts with no links will be deleted.</span><br /><br />Ready!  Set!   Go!</p>
