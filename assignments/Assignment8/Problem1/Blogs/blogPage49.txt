<div>Mystic Chords Of Memory is a place where die-hard music fans can find rare &amp; non-commercial recordings of their favorite artists. Commercial material is not available on this blog and I do not link to blogs that share albums &amp; DVDs illegally. This is a non-profit blog and all requests asking me to promote upcoming albums, tours, etc. will be denied.</div><div><br/></div><div>If you are affiliated with a band or artist and do not want your recordings shared on Mystic Chords Of Memory, please contact me at (email removed due to spam) and I will take the recording down immediately. If a recording is pulled I will not re-up it. I figure that someone (the band, their management, record label, etc.) did not want it shared and I will abide by their wishes.</div><div><br/></div><div>My hope is that people will be exposed to music that they fall in love with and motivates them to take a trip to their local record store and buy their albums, DVDs and see them in concert when possible. I love music and am so very thankful that people record shows and post them online. Because of those people, my musical tastes have been broadened and my life forever changed and I hope the little bit I do here does the same for others.</div><br/>
