<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://davecromwellwrites.blogspot.com/2016/03/caught-live-b-52s-baby-acid-sharkmuffin.html'>Caught Live: The B-52s, Baby Acid, Sharkmuffin, Citris</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4952981865141179917' itemprop='description articleBody'>
Timing and opportunity is a frequent catalyst leading to attendance at compelling events.  Living within a stone&#8217;s throw of The Space At Westbury assures that venue&#8217;s neon-flashing coming events marquee creates a level of &#8220;must-be-there&#8221; desire that might otherwise elude.  So it stood to reason that the months long lead-up hype to truly legendary band The <b>B-52</b>&#8217;s appearance there on <a href="http://thespaceatwestbury.com/?page=show&amp;sid=1263">February 26, 2016</a> was certainly not to be missed.<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-HsAMkzBuesc/VudaWx9b2oI/AAAAAAAAOdk/0S6u77pM14UcQlemsVrqrAsDQ9U6M7F8A/s1600/0001-b52s-02-26-16.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="174" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-HsAMkzBuesc/VudaWx9b2oI/AAAAAAAAOdk/0S6u77pM14UcQlemsVrqrAsDQ9U6M7F8A/s320/0001-b52s-02-26-16.JPG" width="320" /></a></div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: center;">
Hailing from <i>Athens, Georgia</i>, the band formed in 1976.</div>
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-XmJfBBUMpo4/Vudd_5wRR6I/AAAAAAAAOdw/G8wYLA8L81U-FB9kE9SgZYkEdv7U7MN3g/s1600/00010-B52s-02-26-16.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="197" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-XmJfBBUMpo4/Vudd_5wRR6I/AAAAAAAAOdw/G8wYLA8L81U-FB9kE9SgZYkEdv7U7MN3g/s320/00010-B52s-02-26-16.JPG" width="320" /></a></div>
<br />
<br />
Having paved the way for the entire Athens, Georgia scene that followed them at the moment they broke through the NYC club scene and eventually on MTV, the band carries on with original front vocalists <i>Cindy Wilson</i> (vocals, bongos, tambourine), <i>Fred Schneider</i> (vocals, percussion, keyboards) and <i>Kate Pierson</i> (organ, keyboards, bass, vocals),<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-hfAcCtvQYUY/VudfrxuMlsI/AAAAAAAAOd8/AZzu6l2cxx40ZBjqHU9qC2AGetv5ohPZw/s1600/0001-Their-Facebook-Page-Banner-Screencap-02-26-16.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="198" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-hfAcCtvQYUY/VudfrxuMlsI/AAAAAAAAOd8/AZzu6l2cxx40ZBjqHU9qC2AGetv5ohPZw/s320/0001-Their-Facebook-Page-Banner-Screencap-02-26-16.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<br />
The tragic death of initial band visionary (and Cindy's brother) <i>Ricky Wilson</i> and recent retiring from live performing (though technically still a band member) <i>Keith Strickland</i> has the band touring with a compliment of eminently capable long-time side musicians.<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-slxsJTPylR8/Vudf-exkmTI/AAAAAAAAOeA/ci4XcP1y_Pw2fZavQq0wQIodG00ej7HFg/s1600/0005-wall-album-display-02-26-16.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="320" src="https://4.bp.blogspot.com/-slxsJTPylR8/Vudf-exkmTI/AAAAAAAAOeA/ci4XcP1y_Pw2fZavQq0wQIodG00ej7HFg/s320/0005-wall-album-display-02-26-16.jpg" width="218" /></a></div>
<br />
Walls inside <b>The Space At Westbury</b> are decorated with album covers from a vintage era of mid-1970's through 1980's that accurately reflects this venue's intended audience .<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-WSGMduJesds/Vudgk_zrqJI/AAAAAAAAOeM/PfkQMFLxyuMwHHtmN6iEcFs73oE_yEOJg/s1600/0006-wall-album-display-02-26-16.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="320" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-WSGMduJesds/Vudgk_zrqJI/AAAAAAAAOeM/PfkQMFLxyuMwHHtmN6iEcFs73oE_yEOJg/s320/0006-wall-album-display-02-26-16.jpg" width="175" /></a></div>
<br />
Since 12'' vinyl albums having emerged as a revived format among many of the current generation of new musicians, perhaps a future wall like this will exist in a venue 30 + years from now.<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

<br />
What initially propelled this band from obscurity and out-of-the-pack was a visionary combination of kitchy visual styling and a sound blending dance music with surf rock.<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-aNjj6PeA7cE/Vud6TI1Q9qI/AAAAAAAAOfI/vZ2QUiPw2aMYr4GAynqlxxHD8xSIn5YRw/s1600/00012-magic-hands-pic-in-pic-02-26-16.JPG" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="216" src="https://2.bp.blogspot.com/-aNjj6PeA7cE/Vud6TI1Q9qI/AAAAAAAAOfI/vZ2QUiPw2aMYr4GAynqlxxHD8xSIn5YRw/s320/00012-magic-hands-pic-in-pic-02-26-16.JPG" width="320" /></a></div>
<br />
<br />
Further setting them apart from generic copycat bands were guitarists <i>Ricky Wilson</i> and writing counterpart <i>Keith Strickland</i>'s creative approach to guitar tunings. &nbsp;Much like <i>Keith Richards</i> with <b>The Rolling Stones</b>, Wilson devised his own open tunings, creating a unique sound that can be heard here:<br />
<br />
<iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/jxTBYkI6wmc" width="420"></iframe><br />
<br />
<br />
&nbsp;Grouping the strings of his Mosrite guitar into a bass course (usually tuned to fifths for strumming) and a treble course (often tuned in unison), he removed the middle two D + G strings from his playing entirely, although there were times when he did play with five strings. Since the band originally didn't have a bassist, Wilson unified his tunings with a keyboard bass played by Pierson, creating a solid rock and roll sound without a bass guitar.<br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-8gWa9flJO0E/Vul98jBtDaI/AAAAAAAAOfk/29foE1XOGVsS8l4IxCSggzseFK0dPKrgQ/s1600/00013-fred-vin%252Bkate-02-26-16.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="237" src="https://3.bp.blogspot.com/-8gWa9flJO0E/Vul98jBtDaI/AAAAAAAAOfk/29foE1XOGVsS8l4IxCSggzseFK0dPKrgQ/s320/00013-fred-vin%252Bkate-02-26-16.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
