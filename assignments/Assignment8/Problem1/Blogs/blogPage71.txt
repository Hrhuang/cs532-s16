<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-zhJxlYByapc/Vqp3o5YcTBI/AAAAAAAAAQ8/N0Lj1IjRsDo/s1600/blogger-image-1494425039.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="69" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-zhJxlYByapc/Vqp3o5YcTBI/AAAAAAAAAQ8/N0Lj1IjRsDo/s320/blogger-image-1494425039.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
Today we woke up at the crack of dawn (around 8:00 am) to do some more filming at Hillifields. However, it was freezing cold and the whole place had a layer of thick frost. Due to this reason we thought it would be too risky to film (especially since we are all recovering from colds) and the weather condition were not right, not fitting in with our video.
