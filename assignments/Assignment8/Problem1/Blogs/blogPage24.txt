<div style="text-align: justify;">
Seattle singer/songwriter Scott Yoder has released his debut album Looking Back In Blue on Italian label Annibale Records. </h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-73442363203223154' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="Dilettante cover art" height="320" src="https://f4.bcbits.com/img/a3268925911_16.jpg" width="320" /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
NYC based band Toot Sweet have released their sophomore album Dilettane. The thirteen tracks have a theatrical feel to them, that unfortunately comes across as over produced lounge music. The band calls itself cabaret soul and while this is an apt description, the tracks have little for the listener to connect with. There will certainly be those that are drawn to this album, but we find it to be pretentious and lacking of direction. Stream and buy Dilettante at the link below.</div>

This is an EP that stands on it own strengths, while at the same time, it shows a band that should move from the shadows of imitation and find their own sound to embrace. Lush, atmospheric and moving, Twin Haus deliver us a must have EP. Stream and buy Nothing Lavish at the link below.</div>

<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-6973533677447551238' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="The Leave of Leaves cover art" height="320" src="https://f1.bcbits.com/img/a1600951301_16.jpg" width="320" /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Our friends at North Carolina's Silber Records have released The Leave of Leaves, the new album from Siberian artist Thorn1, aka Evgeny Zheyda. This is a record where the isolation of western Siberia is transformed into dark, compelling post-punk songs. The seven tracks are anthemic sojourns juxtaposed with melodic pop passages. Musically varied, starkly beautiful and sonically compelling, Zheyda is an artist whose music is a testament to the creativity to be found in the most unlikely of places. This EP is a definate must have. Stream and buy The leave of leaves at the link below.</div>

</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-3569552174801090466' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="The Bordello underground tape vol 4 cover art" height="320" src="https://f1.bcbits.com/img/a3824659911_16.jpg" width="320" /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
UK garage punk outfit, The Bordellos, have released Vol. 4 of their Underground Tape Series. Stark and edgy, with influences ranging from punk to folk, the four track EP is a swift kick in the pants to the sterile formula indie music that so dominates the blogosphere now. The tracks explore sounds as smooth as dreampop and as raw as full on punk. The Bordellos have always been a band of intensity and originality, and Vol. 4 continues their penchent for life on the ragged edge. Stream and download Vol. 4 for free at the link below.</div>

</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-8709816192618943031' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="Ice in the Belly, Fire in the Mind cover art" height="320" src="https://f1.bcbits.com/img/a0865142861_16.jpg" width="320" /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Swedish synthpop group A Nighthawk have released their debut full length, Ice in the Belly, Fire in the Mind, on their own imprint, Dead Dandelion Collective. Sweden has a legacy of fine music and this places an unnatural burden on any artists coming from this creative land. The duo of Sarah Snavely and Ted Malmros rise to this challenge with an album encompassing a deep musical identity, superlative performances and arrangements, and production that engages the listener intensely with every note. Beautiful, atmospheric and lush, A Nighthawk have given us an album that is a definite must have. Watch the video for album track, Coming For You, then stream and buy Ice in the Belly, Fire in the Mind at the link below.<br />

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://floorshimezipperboots.blogspot.com/2016/03/guy-grogan-dynamite-bouquet.html'>Guy Grogan: Dynamite Bouquet</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-744216210595417731' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="dynamite bouquet cover art" height="320" src="https://f1.bcbits.com/img/a0738872248_16.jpg" width="320" /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Santa Fe based singer/songwriter Guy Grogan has released his new album, Dynamite Bouquet. The award wining songwriter serves up ten indie rock tracks that draw comparisons to the likes of Dinosaur JR and Guided By Voices. This is an album of two personalities. The first, of deep lyrical prowess, while the second, sees an album that lacks connection with contemporary indie music, having a sound firmly ensconced in the past. The production, unfortunately, seems to take all the edge out of the record. You know it's there, however the sonic experience is flat and lacks the rawness or power that defines truly compelling indie rock. Stream and buy Dynamite Bouquet at the link below.</div>

<div style="text-align: justify;">
Michigan based artist/producer Dominic Coppola is back with Ladylike, his new two track EP. Spanning 22 minutes of music, Coppola utilizes soothing tonal drones to paint an ambient mindscape of love restored. The EP is available as a cassette from Bent Crayon Records. Stream and buy Ladylike at the link below.</div>

</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5200111553370555116' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="Walk Away cover art" height="320" src="https://f1.bcbits.com/img/a2185843086_16.jpg" width="320" /><img alt="Be My Friend cover art" height="320" src="https://f1.bcbits.com/img/a3113991641_16.jpg" width="320" /></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
To mark FZB's 5th birthday, we have a two single releases from our parent label Russian Winter Records. The first is Walk Away, by artist/producer Ric Gordon. The raw post-punk track is an edgy protest song that paints a stark picture of where America finds itself now, as a country of corporate greed, elitism, blatant racism, discrimination of LGBT and older people, and an out of control police. The second, Be My Friend is the new single from garage punk artist Braggers. Lo-fi and more pop than previous releases, this track sees Braggers exploring realms that meld together influences from Roxy Music to Wavves. Both tracks are must haves. Stream and buy Walk Away and Be My Friend at the links below.</div>
