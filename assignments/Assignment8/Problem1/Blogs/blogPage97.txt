<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://timeispoetry.blogspot.com/2016/02/our-hue.html'>Our Hue</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5090702355416035731' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">The earth is such a tiny home</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">that nurtures hearts immense.</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">Deep into the dark, I roam,</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">dancing the great expanse.</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">The universe is fully known,</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">every careless cosmic romance,</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">so don't ever think that you're alone.</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">Nothing here is left to chance.</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">So, look up in your starry sky.<br />I twinkle just for you.</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">And how I wish that you could fly;</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">Oh, how you'd love the view.</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">Above the clouds, you could be mine</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">and I would be yours too</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif; font-size: x-small;">and those below would see our shine;</span></div>
<div>
<span style="font-family: Georgia, Times New Roman, serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">they'd shimmer in our hue.</span><br /><br /><span style="font-size: x-small;">******************************</span><wbr></wbr><span style="font-size: x-small;">****************</span><br /><br /><span style="font-size: xx-small;"><br /><br />"You
 couldn't be more beautiful to me. There's no where in this world I'd 
rather be. And now I know that everything's all right if we stay between
 the glow and the light." <b>Jump, Little Children </b><i>Dim and the Dark</i></span></span></div>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://timeispoetry.blogspot.com/2016/01/longing.html'>Longing</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2302571920920599060' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; display: inline ! important; float: none; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">I don't want it to hurt;</span></span></span><br />
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">I want it to be beautiful.</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">I want the cool cheeks</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">of our calm autumn nights,</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">"I love yous" in the rustling</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">of newly fallen leaves,</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">secrets whispered&nbsp;</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">through the trustworthy breeze,</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">and that way the moon looks at me -</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">like the longing lover lost.</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">***************************************************</span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><span style="font-size: x-small;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="color: #222222; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;"><br /></span></span></div>
<div style="-webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: small; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: normal; orphans: auto; text-align: start; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; white-space: normal; widows: 1; word-spacing: 0px;">
<span style="font-size: xx-small;"><span style="font-family: Georgia,&quot;Times New Roman&quot;,serif;">"And anyway I just wanted to show you some happiness. I wanted to show you the stars. I want to show you the stars. And we go." <b>Aerogramme</b> <i>In Gratitude </i></span></span></div>
