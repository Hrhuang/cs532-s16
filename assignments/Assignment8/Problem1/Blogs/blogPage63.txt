<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://pithytitlehere.blogspot.com/2015/12/the-best-albums-of-2015.html'>The Best Albums of 2015</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4863115654670252715' itemprop='description articleBody'>
So, this morning, the new Coldplay album dropped. I can never hear every album in a given year and there&#39;s always something everyone else agrees is amazing and puts on their lists that I discover too late and, yeah... but the Coldplay album was one I had pre-ordered and we&#39;re talking about, possibly, the biggest revenue band in the world right now. I&#39;ve only listened a couple times and I don&#39;t have nearly enough time to let it settle in and see where it might fit in with the picks below. <i>That said</i>... when you say &quot;featuring Tove Lo&quot; in the song title and you really mean &quot;she does the backing vocals,&quot; you aren&#39;t playing your strongest hand by definition.<br>
<br>
Anyhow... 2015 was just plain lousy with good music and I kind of don&#39;t want the year to end if there&#39;s some sort of magic in the air this calendar year that makes amazing happen.<br>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry uncustomized-post-template' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://pithytitlehere.blogspot.com/2015/08/coming-to-terms-with-guns-everywhere.html'>Coming to terms with guns everywhere</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-8058641361886798275' itemprop='description articleBody'>
On July 20, 2012, I posted <a href="http://pithytitlehere.blogspot.com/2012/07/accountability-in-our-sights.html" target="_blank">this</a>. That day, someone walked into an Aurora, CO movie theater and, instead of buying a popcorn and watching a movie, that someone killed 12 people and injured 70 others. He fired 76 shots in, roughly, 90 seconds.<br>
<br>
In that blog post, after pointing out we have more firearms than cars in this country, I asked a simple question: &quot;If people want their guns, what are they willing to accept as a result?&quot;<br>
<br>
Less than six months later, it took another shooter less than five minutes to kill 20 children and six adults at Connecticut&#39;s Sandy Hook Elementary School. Since then, the answer to my question is painfully, awfully clear.<br>
<br>
Our American culture, when the votes are tallied, will accept absolutely anything to preserve not only gun ownership, but also some intangible aspect of &quot;freedom&quot; that exists almost nowhere else in our culture.<br>
<br>
