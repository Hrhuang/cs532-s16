This week&#8217;s GLI Press Song of the Week is &#8220;Seasons Change&#8221; by <a href="https://nadiareid.bandcamp.com/" target="_blank">Nadia Reed</a>. This track in particular sounds like a Country-and-Westernized Mazzy Star, ghostly and rustic at the same time.<br />

<b><u><br /></u></b>
<b><u><span style="font-size: large;">Other Highlights:</span></u></b><br />
<br />
The band <a href="http://www.gumtakestooth.com/" target="_blank">Gum Takes Booth</a> has a very interesting audio setup. The drums and the other instruments are connected together, with the not-drum instruments being triggered by the striking of a particular drum and processed by the other musicians. It&#8217;s an ingenious setup that produces a raw, relentless and engrossing techno sound.<br />

<h3 class='post-title entry-title'>
<a href='http://glipress.blogspot.com/2016/02/gli-press-song-of-week-sittin-by-radio.html'>GLI Press Song of the Week: "Sittin' By the Radio" by Adrian Younge</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-7105662939152043642'>
<div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
<div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
<i>The GLI Press Song of the Week roams the internet looking for the best in shared new music.</i><br />
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-CHSy6uWu5iw/Vq6Z8J_F4wI/AAAAAAAAFUY/HOdc2NxKsk4/s1600/Adrian%2BYounge.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="320" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-CHSy6uWu5iw/Vq6Z8J_F4wI/AAAAAAAAFUY/HOdc2NxKsk4/s320/Adrian%2BYounge.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<br />
This week&#8217;s GLI Press Song of the Week is &#8220;Sittin&#8217; By the Radio&#8221; by composer and producer <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adrian_Younge" target="_blank">Adrian Younge</a>. Strictly Old School Soul from a guy who knows how to get it done (he's been producing records since 2000). Pretty much everything about his sounds and image will give you an itch to seek out more of his work or go out and find some of the &#8216;60s and &#8216;70s Soul magic that clearly inspires him.<br />
<br />
<br />
<audio controls="">
  <source src="http://download.publicradio.org/podcast/minnesota/the_current/song_of_the_day/2016/01/28/20160128_adrian_younge_sittin_by_the_radio_128.mp3" type="audio/mpeg"></source>
  <source src="http://download.publicradio.org/podcast/minnesota/the_current/song_of_the_day/2016/01/28/20160128_adrian_younge_sittin_by_the_radio_128.ogg" type="audio/ogg"></source>
  <embed height="50" src="http://download.publicradio.org/podcast/minnesota/the_current/song_of_the_day/2016/01/28/20160128_adrian_younge_sittin_by_the_radio_128.mp3" width="100"></embed>
</audio><br />
<i>(audio source courtesy of <a href="http://minnesota.publicradio.org/" target="_blank">Minnesota Public Radio</a>)</i>
<br />
SoTW004<br />
<b><u><br /></u></b>
<b><u><span style="font-size: large;">Other Highlights:</span></u></b><br />
<br />
Here&#8217;s an excellent track by the Montreal duo <a href="https://she-devilsinc.bandcamp.com/releases" target="_blank">She-Devils</a> from their debut EP. They remind me of Sleigh Bells in how they combine noise with a 1950s/1960s pop sensibility, but they do it in a different way that&#8217;s just as provocative, only more subtle.<br />
<br />
<iframe allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/1wVYjnlgP7E" width="560"></iframe><br />

</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-5566880793830847286'>
<div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on">
Last night on his show, <a href="http://www.cbs.com/shows/the-late-show-with-stephen-colbert/" target="_blank">Stephen Colbert</a> showed just how hypocritical Donald Trump is in the political arena. It&#8217;s pretty straightforward &#8211; just show a recording of Trump talking, then show another shot of him stating the exact opposite. Repeat over and over. Trump has always been known as a gasbag who will say anything as long as it&#8217;s to his advantage. This is just more documented proof.<br />
