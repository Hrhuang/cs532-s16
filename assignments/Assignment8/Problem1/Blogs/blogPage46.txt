Guitarist Thomas Fekete and bassist Kevin Williams are no longer in the band &#8211; Fekete battles a rare form of cancer while Williams left the group for a job &#8211; but their replacements Mike McCleary and Lindsey Mills proved ample replacements. GrimyGoods.com<br />

Is there anyone in music who&#8217;s had a bigger 2015 than The Weeknd?<br />
<br />
Wednesday night&#8217;s show at The Forum &#8211; the second of two sold-out shows on The Madness Tour at the 17,500 capacity venue &#8211; was further evidence that the answer to that question is no.<br />
<br />
With a nearly two-hour set, The Weeknd &#8211; aka Abel Tesfaye &#8211; proved his merit and vocal prowess. Perhaps the highlight of the hit-filled set was the shocking surprise in the form of a Lana del Rey appearance at the end of his set to perform their track &#8220;Prisoner&#8221; from his critically-acclaimed 2015 record Beauty Behind the Madness.<br />
