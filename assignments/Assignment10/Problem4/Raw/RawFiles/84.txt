		<p>The Dallas Stars scored a late winner, held on in the final minute and eventually struck first in their best-of-seven second-round series with the St. Louis Blues.</p>
<p>Once again, it was the speed and skill of the Stars that proved to be the difference in the end. <a href="http://www.rotoworld.com/player/nhl/4037/radek-faksa" target="_blank">Radek Faksa</a> scored with less than five minutes remaining in the third period, breaking the deadlock and giving Dallas a 2-1 victory and 1-0 series lead over their Central Division foes on Friday.</p>
<p>As he entered the zone on the rush, Faksa dished off to a flying <a href="http://www.rotoworld.com/player/nhl/1418/ales-hemsky" target="_blank">Ales Hemsky</a>, who was denied by <a href="http://www.rotoworld.com/player/nhl/2388/brian-elliott" target="_blank">Brian Elliott</a> in alone. But Faksa followed up, jamming in the rebound to give the Stars the lead, as both St. Louis defensemen <a href="http://www.rotoworld.com/player/nhl/1542/jay-bouwmeester" target="_blank">Jay Bouwmeester</a> and <a href="http://www.rotoworld.com/player/nhl/2555/alex-pietrangelo" target="_blank">Alex Pietrangelo</a> were caught by the speed of the Dallas forwards on the rush.</p>
<div class="nbcsports-video-wrapper"><iframe src="http://vplayer.nbcsports.com/p/BxmELC/nbcsports_embed/select/media/ZUepIcLVZXgE?autoPlay=false" width="651" height="365" frameborder="0" scrolling="no" allowfullscreen="true" class="nbcsports-video"></iframe></div>
<p>The Stars held on from there, as the Blues made a late push to tie the game.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.rotoworld.com/player/nhl/1536/kari-lehtonen" target="_blank">Kari Lehtonen</a> stopped 31 of 32 shots for Dallas, while Elliott was busy throughout the night, stopping 40 of 42 shots.</p>
<p>Elliott was <a href="http://nhl.nbcsports.com/2016/04/29/video-roussel-opens-the-scoring-for-dallas-and-elliott-wasnt-happy-about-it/" target="_blank">furious after the Stars opened the scoring</a> in the second period, as <a href="http://www.rotoworld.com/player/nhl/3823/antoine-roussel" target="_blank">Antoine Roussel</a> tallied on a rebound after yet another nice Dallas passing play in the offensive zone.</p>
<p>Stars forward <a href="http://www.rotoworld.com/player/nhl/1666/patrick-eaves" target="_blank">Patrick Eaves</a> left the game early in the third period and didn&#8217;t play another shift after being hit in the lower part of his leg with the puck from a point shot.</p>
