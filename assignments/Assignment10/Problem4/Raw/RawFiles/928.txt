<p>Forget Where's Wally – can you spot the zebra in this <a data-action="animals" data-content-type="section-topic" data-track="false" data-type="inline" data-word-count="3" href="http://www.mirror.co.uk/all-about/animals">herd of wildebeest</a>?</p> <p>These incredible photos show a huge herd of wildebeest stampeding through the African bush – but lurking among the pack is a zebra.</p> <p>Despite his distinctive marking, the zebra is almost impossible to pick out among the crowd of wildebeest.</p> <p>The incredible photos of the wildebeest migration were snapped by German wildlife photographer Ingo Gerlach in Maasai Mara, Kenya.</p> <p>Ingo said: &quot;Every year we see this amazing display of nature – but this sight was more than I ever expected.</p> <p>
