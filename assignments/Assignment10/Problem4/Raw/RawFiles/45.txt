<div class="post-text">
<p><span style="line-height: 1.5;">If you've only got 24 hours to find a <a href="http://mashable.com/category/valentine-s-day/">Valentine's Day</a> date, these online dating tips could help.</span></p>
<p>Dating app Hinge looked at 5,000 users who exchanged information within one day of initiating conversation. They determined what opening lines are more likely to get you a phone number, and which to avoid getting ghosted. </p>
<p>Being polite is the obvious answer, but a few simple phrases might either help or hinder your chances.</p>
