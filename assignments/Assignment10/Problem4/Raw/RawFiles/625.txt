	<h1 class="entry-title"><a href="https://mcclellandmiscellanea.wordpress.com/2015/02/04/north-reading-finishes-strong-in-60-56-victory-over-triton/" rel="bookmark">North Reading finishes strong in 60-56 victory over&nbsp;Triton</a></h1>
	<div class="entry entry-content">
		<div data-shortcode="caption" id="attachment_9668" style="width: 510px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://mcclellandmiscellanea.files.wordpress.com/2015/02/notr-a3-adam-goldstein-on-the-right-converted-a-missed-free-throw-to-give-nr-a-58-54-lead.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-9668" src="https://mcclellandmiscellanea.files.wordpress.com/2015/02/notr-a3-adam-goldstein-on-the-right-converted-a-missed-free-throw-to-give-nr-a-58-54-lead.jpg?w=500&#038;h=396" alt="Adam Goldstein (on the right) converted a rebound off a missed free throw to give North Reading a  58-54 lead in the last minute" width="500" height="396" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Adam Goldstein (on the right) converted a rebound off a missed free throw to give North Reading a 58-54 lead in the last minute</p></div>
<div data-shortcode="caption" id="attachment_9670" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a href="https://mcclellandmiscellanea.files.wordpress.com/2015/02/notr-a8-nick-venora-19-points-sees-an-opening.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9670" src="https://mcclellandmiscellanea.files.wordpress.com/2015/02/notr-a8-nick-venora-19-points-sees-an-opening.jpg?w=300&#038;h=167" alt="Nick Venora (19 points) sees an opening" width="300" height="167" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Nick Venora (19 points) sees an opening</p></div>
<p>(Byfield MA) North Reading survived four lead changes in the final quarter and defeated Triton, 60-56, on Wednesday night Cape Ann League action.</p>
<p>The Hornets (7-8) had a very slim, 55-54 lead with 1:12 left but capitalized on a Viking rebounding foul (Luke Reineiger) and a converted offensive rebound (Adam Goldstein) and put five straight points together to win the game.</p>
<p>Triton (7-9) got down early (22-9) as Derek Hogan (18 points) carried North Reading.</p>
<p>Later the Vikings would erase a 30-20 deficit with a 17-1 run that gave the home team a 37-31 lead with three minutes left in the third quarter. Nick Venora (19 points) tallied nine points in a lengthy stretch of solid offense and defense.</p>
<div data-shortcode="caption" id="attachment_9680" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a href="https://mcclellandmiscellanea.files.wordpress.com/2015/02/notr-a10-cam-armand-spins-in.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9680" src="https://mcclellandmiscellanea.files.wordpress.com/2015/02/notr-a10-cam-armand-spins-in.jpg?w=300&#038;h=200" alt="Cam Armand spins in" width="300" height="200" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Cam Armand spins in</p></div>
<p>Cam Armand started the final quarter with a three to get Triton up by four (44-40).</p>
<p>The key to the North Reading win? Twice in the last quarter the Hornets turned misses into point-producing offensive rebounds. The first time it happened senior Gerard Walsh punished Triton with a three after a teammate rebounded Gerard&#8217;s missed free throw to get NR ahead, 52-48. Later it was Adam Goldstein (12 points) converting a missed free throw into two points with a rebound to increase the Hornets&#8217; lead to 58-54 with fifty-four seconds to go.</p>
<p>The Vikings opted for the quick fix 3-point attempts but came up empty on all three tries down the stretch.</p>
<div data-shortcode="caption" id="attachment_9678" style="width: 256px" class="wp-caption alignleft"><a href="https://mcclellandmiscellanea.files.wordpress.com/2015/02/notr-a13-derek-hogan-18-points-drives.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-9678" src="https://mcclellandmiscellanea.files.wordpress.com/2015/02/notr-a13-derek-hogan-18-points-drives.jpg?w=246&#038;h=300" alt="Derek Hogan (18 points) drives" width="246" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Derek Hogan (18 points) drives</p></div>
<p>North Reading won despite twenty turnovers and thirteen (10-for-23) missed free throws. The Hornets miscued on eight freebies in the final quarter.</p>
<p>The Vikings had a very cold shooting night from the field. They did make four 3&#8217;s but needed several more in this one.</p>
<p>North Reading won the first match between the two teams in December, 62-40. Adam Goldstein had 21 points in that NR victory.</p>
<p>The Hornets ended a two-game losing streak. They play Notre Dame Cristo Rey (Lawrence) on Thursday and at undefeated Hamilton-Wenham on Friday.</p>
<p>Hamilton-Wenham routed Triton by thirty-nine points on Friday. Marcus Zegarowski had thirty-one points in that one. HW had only won the previous meeting between the two teams, 51-44, in December.</p>
<p>The Vikings will be at Masco on Friday night.</p>
