<div class="rich-v2 classic no-sidebar clearfix" data-module="article-rich" data-title="Men's track &amp; field to compete Saturday at Colgate" data-url="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/releases/20160208p1pp8q">
  

	
      <h1 class="article-title">Men's track &amp; field to compete Saturday at Colgate</h1>
  

	

<div class="article-body clearfix">
	
			
		
	  	
				
	<div class="article-image">
      <div class="image">
        <img src="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/saucierMTF16a.jpg?max_width=720" alt="Men's track &amp; field to compete Saturday at Colgate" />
  
          
                  <div class="thumb-caption">Eli Saucier '18</div>
          
              </div>
	</div>
  


<div class="article-text">
	
<div class="article-date">
      <div class="date">Feb 09, 2016</div>
    
  	<div class="like-buttons" data-module="share-buttons">
    	  <div class="fb-like-btn"><a class="addthis_button_facebook_like" fb:like:layout="button_count"></a></div>
        	  <div class="tweet-btn"><a class="addthis_button_tweet"></a></div>
    	</div>
  </div>

	
<p>Hamilton College travels to Hamilton, N.Y., on Saturday for Colgate University's Class of '32 Track &amp; Field Invitational. The Hamilton men captured the Class of '32 Invitational team title in 2015.<br>

<br>

 <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/berardelli_dylan_7ewa">Dylan Berardelli</a> '16</strong> and <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/saucier_eli_gv4g">Eli Saucier</a> '18</strong> have led the Continentals so far this indoor season. Berardelli set the school's 35-pound weight throw record last year and has broken it this season several times. Saucier is starting to follow in the footsteps of All-American sprinter Joe Jensen '15, who graduated last spring.<br>

<br>

 Berardelli's latest school-record performance came on Feb. 6 at Utica College's Doris and Walter Miga Invitational, where he took first place out of 23 athletes with a throw of 15.95 meters (52 feet, 4 inches). Last year he finished fifth in the event at the New York State Collegiate Track Conference Indoor Championships and qualified for the Eastern College Athletic Conference (ECAC) Division III Indoor Championships.<br>

<br>

 Saucier is off to a great start in 2016. He finished first out of 35 athletes with a time of 22.48 seconds in the 200-meter dash and was second in the 60-meter dash in 7.12 seconds at Utica College on Saturday. Saucier's performance in the 200 is the 17th-fastest time in Division III as he chases a spot in the 2016 NCAA Division III Men's Indoor Track &amp; Field Championship at Grinnell College in March.<br>

<br>

 Jensen competed in two NCAA indoor championship meets and was a three-time All-American (twice for the 400-meter dash and once for the 200 meters). He finished in fourth place in the 400 meters and fifth in the 200 at the 2015 championship and led Hamilton to a tie for 21st place in the team standings with nine points.<br>

<br>

 Speaking of the national stage, all seven runners who led the Continentals to 21st place at the 2015 NCAA Men's Cross Country Championship are competing this winter. At Utica on Saturday, <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/moses_jack_7o2f">Jack Moses</a> '16</strong> finished third in the 3,000-meter run. <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/pfander_adam_o3nw">Adam Pfander</a> '16</strong> ended up fourth in the mile run and sixth in the 1,000-meter run, <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/sullivan_harry_0aa7">Harry Sullivan</a> '16</strong> was fifth in the 1,000 meters and sixth in the mile, and <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/freeman_david_471x">David Freeman</a> '16</strong> took third place in the 5,000-meter run. The remaining three athletes are <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/pierce_jack_ajeq">Jack Pierce</a> '17, <a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/whipple_henry_ien3">Henry Whipple</a> '18</strong> and <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/abelson_evan_t0th">Evan Abelson</a> '16</strong>.<br>

<br>

 Saucier was part of a 1,600-meter relay that set an indoor school record and finished 11th at the 2015 ECAC championships. <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/somes_nate_ot64">Nate Somes</a> '16, <a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/kilekwang_leonard_92e8">Leonard Kilekwang</a> '16</strong> and Jensen rounded out that foursome. Saucier, Somes, <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/whitney_grant_3i9s">Grant Whitney</a> '17</strong> and <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/mccarron_matthew_44k2">Matthew McCarron</a> '17</strong> posted a victory in the 1,600-meter relay at Utica on Saturday. Whitney was also the runner-up in the 800-meter run.<br>

<br>

 Somes and <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/newman_max_wpn9">Max Newman</a> '16</strong> are part of a school-record 800-meter relay. Whitney and <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/deweirdt_peter_6djh">Peter DeWeirdt</a> '18</strong> ran on a 3,200-meter relay that finished eighth at last year's ECAC championships and earned all-conference team honors. DeWeirdt grabbed third place in the mile at Utica on Saturday.<br>

<br>

 In the field events, Kilekwang is Hamilton's top athlete in the triple jump as he finished third in the event at Utica. <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/gonzalez_rico_j65n">Rico Gonzalez</a> '16</strong> competes in the pole vault and Berardelli ended up in second place in the shot put at Utica.<br>

<br>

 <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/sedwick_ryan_n1wk">Ryan Sedwick</a> '19</strong>&nbsp;provides depth in the sprints as he finished in the top eight in the 60 and 200 meters at Utica. Some other top middle-distance runners include <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/sinclair_andrew_a4f3">Andrew Sinclair</a> '18, <a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/horgan_colin_3tgg">Colin Horgan</a> '19</strong> and <strong><a href="/sports/mtrack/2015-16/bios/shew_reilly_54a6">Reilly Shew</a> '19</strong>.</p>
