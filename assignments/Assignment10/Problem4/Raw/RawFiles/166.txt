          <h1 class="entry-title ">UEFA European Football Club Rankings</h1><hr class="featurette-divider __before_content">        </header>
        
        <div class="entry-content">
          <p>Below are listed the current Top 100 clubs in UEFA as determined by the ratings of the Donchess Inference Index.<br />
More information about how these European football rankings are calculated can be found on the <a href="http://www.dratings.com/methodology/">Methodology</a> page.</p>
<p><span style="color: red;">DII Rating</span>&#8211; This is a European football rankings system that is a combination of the <span style="color: green;">Standard Rating</span> and the <span style="color: orange;">Inference Rating</span>. The Inference Rating is weighted more highly in the beginning of the season whereas the Standard Rating is weighted more highly at the end of the season. This is the final rating that is used to rank the teams.</p>
<p><span style="color: green;">Standard Rating</span>&#8211; Looks at game outcome and score differential only. Record is the most important factor in this rating system.</p>
<p><span style="color: orange;">Inference Rating</span>&#8211; Heavily weights the outcomes of opponents&#8217; games. This is system is a much better predictor of future game outcomes.</p>
