Like when he was drafted by Toronto in 2009, as a 19-year-old college freshman. Now? Now he&rsquo;s 26, a league fixture&nbsp;and a two-time All-Star who has made the city his home. And with the 2016 game in Toronto, he gets to play host.</p>
