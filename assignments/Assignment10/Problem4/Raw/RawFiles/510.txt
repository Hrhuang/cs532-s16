<h1>Lynzi Mueller, Carlow University</h1>

<p><b>Senior &bull; Guard&bull; Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania</b></p>

  <p>This week&rsquo;s USCAA Women&rsquo;s Division I Basketball Player of the Week honor goes <strong>Lynzi Mueller</strong> of <strong>Carlow University</strong>. Against Rio Grande, Mueller led the Celtics with 20 points (6-14 FG's) (3-6 three-point FG's) and 5-6 from the charity stripe. She also pulled down four boards and had two assists to go along with two steals. This past Saturday the Celtics avenged a 20 point loss earlier in the season to Ohio Christian with a 64-57 win. In the victory, Mueller scored 17 points, grabbed five boards, dished out two assists and also tallied two steals. Carlow University is now 11-11 on the season and ranked seventh in the latest USCAA Coaches Poll. &nbsp;</p>
