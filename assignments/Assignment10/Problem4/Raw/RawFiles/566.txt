<p>There was a melee on the streets of Atherstone this afternoon for the <a data-action="10847710" data-content-type="news" data-track="false" data-type="inline" data-word-count="4" href="http://www.coventrytelegraph.net/whats-on/food-drink-news/pancake-day-pancakes-coventry-10847710" target="_blank">817th Shrovetide Ball Game</a>.</p> <p>The rough-and-tumble event, which dates back to the 12th century, was as popular as ever with <a data-action="atherstone" data-content-type="section-topic" data-track="false" data-type="inline" data-word-count="13" href="http://www.coventrytelegraph.net/all-about/atherstone" target="_blank">hundreds of people chasing a 4lb ball around the streets of the town</a> for two hours.</p> <p>The <a data-action="10861521" data-content-type="news" data-track="false" data-type="inline" data-word-count="16" href="http://www.coventrytelegraph.net/whats-on/whats-on-news/what-atherstone-ball-game-everything-10861521" target="_blank">winner is the one with the ball in their hands at the end of the contest</a>.</p> <p>This year’s winner was Jonathan Slessor, of Atherstone, who had the help of the 'Grendon Lads'.</p> <p>Stores boarded up doors and windows, with traffic being diverted to allow teams a free run of the streets.</p> <p>The game was started at Barclays Bank by Tamworth-born footballer Marc Albrighton, currently earning rave reviews <a href="http://www.coventrytelegraph.net/all-about/barclays-premier-league" target="_blank" data-content-type="section-topic" data-type="inline" data-action="barclays-premier-league" data-word-count="6" data-track="false">playing for Premier League table toppers</a> Leicester City.</p> <p>Police presence was at an all time low this year with officers using CCTV to keep an eye on any off-the-ball trouble.</p> <p>Other activities that the crowd took part in were a raffle, which raised &pound;906 for charity, a pancake race and golden penny ball game. </p> <p>
