</div><!-- / hdnce-e --></div><p>Johnny Manziel reportedly showed up to a Cleveland Browns practice inebriated and the team covered it up by announcing that he was undergoing the concussion protocol, <a href="http://www.nfl.com/videos/cleveland-browns/0ap3000000632905/Silver-Manziel-showed-up-drunk-at-practice" target="_blank">according to the NFL Network's Michael Silver</a>.</p>
<p>"I was with a Browns player yesterday. Johnny Manziel, going in to what would have been a start late in the season, showed up drunk at practice on a Wednesday," Silver said on the NFL Network. "The Browns lied and said he was in the concussion protocol. Let me say that again: The Browns lied. To try to protect and, I would argue, enable this irresponsible and very troubled young man."</p><!-- fixed-asset perfectPixelWide --><!-- hearst/item/standalone.tpl -->

<p>The Cleveland Browns denied the report through a statement released Tuesday.</p>
<p>"Johnny Manziel came to our facility on the morning of December 30th and complained of concussion symptoms," the report read. "He was tested by an independent neurologist and entered the league's concussion protocol. He remained in the protocol until January 12th after being cleared by the independent neurologist."</p>
<p>According to Silver, Manziel showed up drunk to practice on Dec. 30. Manziel was ruled out for the game with a concussion and Austin Davis started in his place.</p>

<p>An <a href="http://www.cleveland.com/browns/index.ssf/2016/02/browns_say_johnny_manziel_did.html" target="_blank">NFL spokesperson talked with Mary Kay Cabot of the Cleveland Plain Dealer</a> and&nbsp;said the league wouldn't have any comment on the report.</p>
<p>"Mike Silver doesn't work for the league office. We don't have any comment on this report."</p>
<p>Tuesday afternoon, Silver took to Twitter to defend his report. He tweeted that he regretted using the word "lied" when it came to the Browns, because he doesn't know what Manziel told the team when he showed up late for practice.</p><div class="asset_item asset-item fullwidth" data-config-asset-position="4"><!-- design/article/assets/Freeform.tpl oid:item.52865 mid:freeform.52865 -->
