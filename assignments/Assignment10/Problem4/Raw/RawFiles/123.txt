</a><p class="entry-meta"><time class="entry-time" itemprop="datePublished" datetime="2016-01-30T23:35:37+00:00">January 30, 2016</time> By <span class="entry-author" itemprop="author" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/Person"><a href="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/author/alexzandraodonnell/" class="entry-author-link" itemprop="url" rel="author"><span class="entry-author-name" itemprop="name">Alexzandra O&#039;Donnell</span></a></span> <span class="entry-comments-link"><a href="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/2016/01/big-game-chocolate-champs-no-bake-cookies/#comments">410 Comments</a></span> </p></header><div class="entry-content" itemprop="text"><a id="dd_start"></a><h2>Chocolate Champs No Bake Cookies</h2>
<p><a href="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/2016/01/big-game-chocolate-champs-no-bake-cookies/big-game-chocolate-champs-2_1/" rel="attachment wp-att-30822"><img class="size-full wp-image-30822 aligncenter" src="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Big-Game-Chocolate-Champs-2_1.jpg" alt="Big Game Chocolate Champs 2_1" width="700" height="394" srcset="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Big-Game-Chocolate-Champs-2_1.jpg 700w, http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Big-Game-Chocolate-Champs-2_1-300x169.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px" /></a></p>
<p>Hi, it&#8217;s Still Blonde&#8217;s resident baker, Alexzandra! I&#8217;m back with a wonderful treat for you. Chocolate Champs No Bake Cookies are a Big Game Party treat that’s hard to beat! Quick to make— no-bake! Easy to take-n’-go. Add some flair to your super-bowl party with this easy recipe that will make peanut butter and chocolate lovers craving to know how they are made.</p>
<h3>A Family Favorite for Years!</h3>
<p>My family has made this recipe for YEARS. My siblings and I talk all the time about when we were in elementary all the other students would grab these cookies first at school parties, because Chocolate Champs No Bake Cookies taste SO GOOD. No one else ever brought in a better treat for school events. My dad always helped us make these, with his contribution being to make sure all the ingredients were there because these cookies are SO EASY to make.<br />

Continue in 30 second increments until completely melted.</li> <li class="ERSInstructionImage"><a href="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/?attachment_id=30824"><img src="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Big-Game-Chocolate-Champs-preparation.jpg" width="3180" height="2850" class="alignleft size-full" title="Big Game Chocolate Champs preparation" alt="Big Game Chocolate Champs preparation" /></a></li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">Remove a tray of the prepared Ritz and peanut butter sandwiches from the fridge. Place back on tray to cool.</li> <li class="ERSInstructionImage"><a href="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/?attachment_id=30825"><img src="http://stillblondeafteralltheseyears.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/Big-Game-Chocolate-Champs-preparation-2_1.jpg" width="700" height="447" class="alignleft size-full" title="Big Game Chocolate Champs preparation 2_1" /></a></li> <li class="instruction" itemprop="recipeInstructions">If placing decorative toppers onto cookies, place when chocolate is still slightly melted.
