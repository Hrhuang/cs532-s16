<div id="story-relate-headline" style="display:none"> </div> <div class="story-content"> <p>&nbsp;A non-invasive hyper-cooling treatment is becoming wildly popular among those seeking quick pain relief, a boost of energy and much more.&nbsp; For one well-known local athlete, it&#39;s been life-changing.</p><p>Houston Texans linebacker Brian Cushing is no stranger to hard knocks.&nbsp; He has taken some big hits and it&#39;s taken its toll.</p><p>That&#39;s when other pro athletes told him about cryotherapy.</p><p>Cushing told KPRC Channel 2, &quot;First time I tried it was in 2011. I went to the (Houston) Rockets facility and they were nice enough to let me use it and I just noticed right away how impactful it was.&quot;</p><p><a href="http://glosunspa1.tru-m.com/">Glo Sun Spa </a>co-owner Cristina Cooker said many people are drawn to the cryochamber because it boosts your energy while decreasing inflammation.</p><p>Cooker said, &quot;We have got many people that come in for injuries. So you&#39;ve got muscle, tendon, joint pain.&nbsp; It&#39;s really great for that. It&#39;s really, really good for anti-aging. It boosts your metabolism. From a wellness aspect, it&#39;s really great for stress, anxiety. It helps people with insomnia sleep better.&quot;</p><p>Clients stay in the chamber for up to three minutes, if you can stand it!</p><p>Cooker explained, &quot;(The cryochamber) fills up with nitrogen oxide. That air that surrounds you is negative 220 degrees. It brings your skin down to 30 degrees.&quot;</p><p>The first time was a shocker for Cushing.</p><p>He said, &quot;Cold! Very cold! I was a little shocked how cold it was, but it&#39;s one of those things you get used to as the time went along. The benefits that I got, I thought it was something that was very good for me.&quot;</p><p>Cushing said he steps in the cryochamber about three times a week.</p><p> <div class='mod-wrapper wrapper-media photo right'> <a href="#" data-toggle="modal" data-target="#imageStoryDialog"> <span class="crop-photo" style="background-image: url('http://media.click2houston.com/photo/2016/02/09/cryotherapy%20cushing2_1455023368961_2138297_ver1.0_320_180.jpg');"> <img src="http://media.click2houston.com/photo/2016/02/09/cryotherapy%20cushing2_1455023368961_2138297_ver1.0_320_180.jpg" alt="cryotherapy cushing2_1455023368961.jpg"> </span> </a> </div> </p><p>He explained, &quot;The soreness, the aches, the body aches, the things from playing football and being an athlete were diminishing becoming less and less and that was something I was grateful for.&quot;</p><p>He added, &quot;Just do it.&nbsp; Don&#39;t think too much about it.
