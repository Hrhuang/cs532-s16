			<p><a href="http://www.alphabetagamer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/the-breakfast-club.gif"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-19387" src="http://www.alphabetagamer.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/the-breakfast-club.gif" alt="the breakfast club" width="650" height="366" /></a></p>
<p><em>The Breakfast Club</em> is a very silly game in which you and up to 3 other friends possess everyday objects, such as knives, plates and bread, then attempt to make breakfast.</p>
<p>The current build of <em>The Breakfast Club</em> was created for Global Game Jam 2016, and features one level (called &#8216;Fear &amp; Loafing&#8217;) in which you and your friends attempt to toast and butter 6 slices of bread. Needless to say this is a tricky task and wonderfully chaotic &#8211; too many cooks really do spoil the broth (or toast), but it&#8217;s great fun witnessing the carnage in 4 player multiplayer.</p>
<p>Making breakfast has never been so tricky, but it&#8217;s great fun. With a bit of perseverance and a lot of luck, maybe you&#8217;ll eventually get that toast buttered. We can&#8217;t wait to see the new level the devs are working on, &#8216;Fry Hard&#8217;, there&#8217;s nothing like a good cooked breakfast!</p>
<p>Note:  Struggling to get the toast in the toaster?  You&#8217;ll need to construct some sort of a ramp&#8230;</p>
