      <h1 class="article-title">Men's Golf Takes First in Pnoomonia Invitational</h1>
  

	

<div class="article-body clearfix">
	
			
		
	  


<div class="article-text">
	
<div class="article-date">
      <div class="date">Feb 08, 2016</div>
    
  	<div class="like-buttons" data-module="share-buttons">
    	  <div class="fb-like-btn"><a class="addthis_button_facebook_like" fb:like:layout="button_count"></a></div>
        	  <div class="tweet-btn"><a class="addthis_button_tweet"></a></div>
    	</div>
  </div>

	
<p>VICTORVILLE, Calif. -- College of the Desert men's golf has gotten off to a hot start in its 2016 season, as it took first place with a team score of 201 in yesterday's Pnoomonia Invitational in Victorville.</p>
<p>In the better-ball event that featured three teams of two players from each school, the Roadrunners were led by the sophomore duo of <a href="/sports/mgolf/2015-16/bios/vlassaks_kenzo_1k5y">Kenzo Vlassaks</a> (Hasselt, BEL) and <a href="/sports/mgolf/2015-16/bios/baker_blaine_560r">Blaine Baker</a> (Highland, CA/Citrus Valley HS) who combined to score a 66. &nbsp;Sophomores <a href="/sports/mgolf/2015-16/bios/emery_ryan_9nf4">Ryan Emery</a> (Burford, GA/Granite Hills HS) and <a href="/sports/mgolf/2015-16/bios/starbuck_michael_je5a">Michael Starbuck</a> (Highland, CA/Citrus Valley HS) finished just behind them with a 67, while sophomores <a href="/sports/mgolf/2015-16/bios/schaloum_mike_exxf">Mike Schaloum</a> (Seattle, WA/O'Dea HS) and <a href="/sports/mgolf/2015-16/bios/jackson_sam_t3oe">Sam Jackson</a> (Santa Cruz, CA/Harbor HS) combined to shoot a 68. &nbsp;</p>
<p>College of the Desert will next compete in the Mt. San Jacinto Invitational on Wednesday, February 17. &nbsp;Tee time is set for 9 a.m.</p>
