   There were reviews about this pack being smaller than it appears but

   to detail. However, this is not nearly 15l by any stretch. I'd be
   surprised if it's even 10l. I shopped 15l bags in person before
   ordering this pack and it does not come near the size of any pack I
   handled. I don't know if it compares apples to apples but my 2 liter

   for day hiking. I don't know what they consider a day hike but I
   couldn't fit a fraction of the equipment I bring on a good hike. Rain
   gear, packable jacket, food, water, emergency bivvy, etc. The lack of
   size is unworkable to begin with but the side zipper as opposed to a
   top loader makes me wonder why this bag exists at all. To pack or
   access anything from the side is foolishly awkward. If you want
   somewhere to put your phone, sunglasses and keys buy cargo shorts.

   By [163]Karen B. on April 20, 2015

   I needed a waterproof backpack for walking around a city with a lot of
   precipitation and also for glacier hiking. Based on other reviews, I
   was already expecting it to be on the smaller side but it was even
   smaller than I was expecting. With that being said, the backpack was
   still able to hold everything that I needed for the day. I think the

   sleeve women's shirt, rolled up waterproof women's pants, a small
   wallet, and a small zippered bag for miscellaneous small items. My
   water bottle was held on the side outer pocket. I used this bag in the
   rain and snow and the contents in the main large compartment were kept
   dry; however, the items in the two small zipper pockets on the front
   got wet. It appears that although the material is waterproof, the
   zippers are not. This was not a big deal to me since I didn't store
   anything in those pockets that were really important but it is
   definitely something to keep in mind. The backpack is very comfortable
   to wear and the size prevents you from overloading the bag, which keeps
   it light. The shape of the bag maintains a smaller profile and it
   doesn't protrude so far from the body. The fabric seems durable and
   doesn't look like it would tear easily.

   little different from what I was expecting but it turned out to be a
   great choice for my activities. Although the backpack wasn't completely
   waterproof due to the zippers, I would still recommend this bag and
   enjoyed using it on my trip.
