Clown Standoff

   Designer: Ludovic Barbe
   Artwork: Antonis Papantoniou
   Number of Players: 2 - 6
   Duration: 10min
   Ages: 8+
   Rules: Coming soon! (please check below for an overview)

   Available for pre-order through our store - Estimated delivery time:
   May 2016

   THIS IS NOT AN EXPANSION. IT IS A STAND-ALONE GAME

   Description: The circus show went really really bad and now the clowns
   are blaming each other...by throwing pies!
   Clowns' code dictates that whoever remains clean the longest, will win
   the argument.

   Clown Standoff is an implementation of the game Pen T'es Mort designed
   by Ludovic Barbe.

   [CLOWN%203_Painted.jpg?1455044523950]

   Gameplay: The game is separated into two phases.
   Phase one is a trick-taking phase with simultaneous card selection.
   First we check for ties. The tie numbers are gone and then the highest
   card wins. Cards are 1 to 6. Four times per card.
   If after the first check we have a 1 card and a 6, then the 1 card wins
   the trick.
   So now all keep the winning trick cards and we are ready for phase 2.
   In Phase 2 we have 6 cards (Pie Throw cards) in the middle of the
   table, 5 "Dodge" cards and a "Hit" card.
   On your turn (beginning with the player with the most cards from phase
   one):
   You can draw a card or you can discard one card from the ones you
   collected in Phase one, to shuffle all open Pie Throw cards back
   together and then draw one, or you can discard cards equal to the
   number of players who are still in the game to pass your turn to the
   next player.
   If a player draws a Dodge card, nothing happens and the remaining cards
   move to the next player. If a player draws a Hit card, that player is
   out of the game. All Pie Throw cards are again shuffled to form a deck
   and the player with the most cards from Phase one starts a new turn.

   Last man standing is the winner!
