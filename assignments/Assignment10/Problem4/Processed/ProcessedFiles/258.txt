Show Notes:

     * Today I talk about the painful moment when you have to stay in the
       situation that you’re in and figure out how to “manage the waiting
       game.”
          + The four emotions to watch for and manage.
     * #1 Keep Focused on the Big Picture
     * #2 Create Systems for Success
     * #3 Be in the Moment
