   SAN DIEGO (AP) -- Skip Schumaker has agreed to a minor league contract
   with the [87]San Diego Padres and will be invited to big league spring
   training.

   The 36-year-old became a free agent after Cincinnati declined a $2.5
   million club option in November.

   If he makes the club, Schumaker would provide a left-handed bat off the
   bench. He has played all three outfield positions and at second base.

   Schumaker also played for St. Louis and the [88]Los Angeles Dodgers.

   San Diego announced the agreement Tuesday.
