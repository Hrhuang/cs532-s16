   "Hey you! Yes. You. The one staring at these words with that weird look

Customer Reviews

Great game but annoying


   by Physbeam

   This game is awesome and is a must get! My only issue is the fact that
   I can't get lives for watching ads anymore, but they still make me
   watch an ad after five deaths. That is annoying!!! Its no fun waiting
   for a day to get 25 lives. I know you can replay for 5 lives but I've
   played the first 24 levels soooo much I actually have memorized the
   levels... Must fix this 10 lives for ad thing

Fun, but annoying.


   by xis10s

   Some levels are incredibly difficult and offer a decent challenge. But
   whenever you run out of lives to keep playing, the game makes you spend
   money for more lives which will last 5 minutes tops or watch a crappy
   video for 10 lives which last no time at all. Without the life system
   it would be infinitely more fun, and you wouldn't feel like your
   wasting your time with videos and purchases and forget what your even
   doing in the app.

The only mobile game I've ever truly enjoyed


   by general_weezer

   I'm not a fan of mobile games, especially given their track record, but
   this one absolutely rocks. It's well made, the ad system is well
   integrated, the gameplay is engaging, and the challenges keep getting
   more challenging. 10/10
