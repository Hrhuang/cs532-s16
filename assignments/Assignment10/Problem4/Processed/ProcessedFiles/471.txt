   Calling all football fans: get closer to the game than ever before with
   E. Sanders! This simple app allows you to personally connect with star
   receiver Emmanuel Sanders and receive a unique digital message and
   signature. Simply upload your fan photo and a message to Emmanuel and
   he’ll respond with a personalized autograph, delivered straight to your
   mobile device.

   We use Touch ID technology to verify that each signature and message
   comes directly from Emmanuel, so this is a great opportunity to show
   off your team pride to the man himself. Upload your game day action
   shot or a pic of you and your friends representing in team gear. Easy
   share functionality makes it simple to prove your ultimate fan status
   across all social media streams. Also makes a great gift: give your
   friends a personalized shout-out from Emmanuel.

   Bring professional football into your social life with E. Sanders!
