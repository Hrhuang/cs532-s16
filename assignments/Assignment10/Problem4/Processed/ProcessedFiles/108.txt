NBC catches Leicester fever in new video to promote Sunday’s game vs. Arsenal

   [118]Christopher Harris February 9, 2016 [119]Arsenal, [120]Leagues:
   EPL, [121]Leicester City, [122]NBC Sports [123]2 Comments

   [124]leicester-promo-video-nbc

   NBC Sports has released a promotional video to build up the momentum
   ahead of Sunday’s Premier League match between Arsenal and Leicester
   City.

   The Foxes take on the Gunners in a game that will be televised live
   across the United States on NBCSN beginning at 7am ET (pre-match
   coverage begins at 6am ET).

   NBC’s new promo video is entitled ‘Who’s Laughing Now?’

   After Sunday’s result, we’ll have a much clearer picture.
