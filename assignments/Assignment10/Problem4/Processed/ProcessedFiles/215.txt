Tabletop Game Giveaways and Contests 1000 Member Prize Celebration!

   [48][bP5BcmSRID0.jpg]
   I manage the Tabletop Game Giveaways and Contests group on Facebook:
   [49]https://www.facebook.com/groups/TabletopGameGiveaways/  Nearly
   every day members of the group post new giveaways for tabletop games
   (and related items).  It's a thriving community of people sharing
   information about the games they are passionate about and ways to win
   those games from the incredibly generous publishers, bloggers, and
   other members of the tabletop games community.
   We just hit 1000 members in the group as a little celebration I am
   organizing a big giveaway of a bunch of items.  I've reached out to a
   number of sponsors and we have game prizes coming in!  This celebration
   giveaway will run for the month of February As more prizes are donated
   to the giveaway I'll list them below.  At the end of the month I'll
   draw names from all the entrants for all the prizes!
   Remember, sponsors are encouraged to enter too!  They just can't win
   their own prizes!  Thank you to everyone who is participating!
   Enter Below the Prize List!
