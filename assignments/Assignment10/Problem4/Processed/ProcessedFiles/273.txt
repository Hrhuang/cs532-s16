Love it, but stability it terrible


   by J.Vis

   I love this game, but the stability of the game is not good. Crashing
   is one thing, but the times that this game crashes are extremely
   annoying.
   Almost all the crashes happen with the video prompts, I don't mind
   watching a 15-60 second add, that's fine. But the fact that it does one
   when you're submitting tournament results--and then freezes, is
   RIDICULOUS. You don't get any credit for your submission, it's like you
   didn't play it at all. And it's worse cause you have no idea if it's
   going to happen, and playing a full tournament is a 45 minute
   investment.
   FIX IT. Or at least eliminate the 'video' after submitting a tournament
   result.
