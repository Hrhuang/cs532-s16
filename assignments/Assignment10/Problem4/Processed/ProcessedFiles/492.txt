   Treasure POP is a bubble shooting game. Throw and match colored stones
   to pop and drop treasures in this challenging bubble shooter with
   addictive and fun gameplay to defeat the Evil Dwarf and recover your
   treasures.

   FEATURES
   - Tricky puzzle gameplay with unlimited FREE Play and 400+ levels.
   - Easy pick up and play for fans of classic bubble shooter games.
   - Match and pop stones to get your gems back from the Evil Dwarf
   - See friends progress and challenge them by connecting to Facebook
   - Awesome graphics that will leave you spellbound
   - Special boosters & God powers to help you pass those extra tricky
   levels
   - Available on mobile and tablet devices

   HOW TO PLAY
   - Shoot, match and pop stones of same color.
   - Use boosters to ease gameplay (Rainbow Stone, Super Aim, Extra Swap &
   Hammer of God)
   - Fill God powers by popping same colored stones. (Red, Blue, Yellow,
   Green and combine them to use Hammer of God).

   GOOD TO KNOW
   Free & easy to play, challenging to master! Treasure Pop is 100% free
   and requires no in-app purchases nor do we require you to connect to
   social networks in order to progress throughout the game. We do however
   allow our players to connect to social networks such as Facebook and
   Twitter to share progress.
